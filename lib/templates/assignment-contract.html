Terms and Conditions of Assignment

1. ASSIGNMENT. For good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is hereby acknowledged, Assignor hereby assigns, transfers, and conveys to Assignee all of Assignor's right, title, and interest in and to that certain Real Estate Purchase Agreement (the "Original Agreement") between Assignor, as Buyer, and {{seller_name}}, as Seller, for the purchase of the above-described Property.

2. ASSIGNMENT FEE. Assignee agrees to pay Assignor an assignment fee of $ {{assignment_fee}} (the "Assignment Fee"). The Assignment Fee shall be due and payable at closing through escrow. The Assignment Fee is non-refundable except in the event Seller defaults under the Original Agreement or the transaction fails to close through no fault of Assignee.

3. ASSUMPTION OF OBLIGATIONS. Assignee hereby assumes and agrees to perform all of the obligations of Assignor under the Original Agreement, including but not limited to:

  • Payment of the purchase price of $ {{purchase_price}} to Seller at closing;
  • Compliance with all terms, conditions, and contingencies set forth in the Original Agreement;
  • Timely performance of all duties and obligations required of Buyer under the Original Agreement;
  • Payment of all closing costs assigned to Buyer under the Original Agreement.

4. SELLER CONSENT. This Assignment is subject to the Seller's consent to the assignment, if required under the Original Agreement. Assignor agrees to use reasonable efforts to obtain such consent if necessary.

5. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES. Assignor represents and warrants to Assignee that: (a) Assignor has full right and authority to assign the Original Agreement; (b) The Original Agreement is in full force and effect and has not been modified; (c) Assignor is not in default under the Original Agreement; (d) Assignor has provided Assignee with a true and complete copy of the Original Agreement.

6. INDEMNIFICATION. Assignor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless Assignee from any claims, damages, or expenses arising from any breach of Assignor's representations and warranties herein.

7. CLOSING. The closing of the transaction shall take place on or before {{close_of_escrow}} at the offices of {{escrow_agent_name}} or such other place as the parties may mutually agree.

8. AS-IS CONDITION. Assignee acknowledges that Assignor makes no representations or warranties regarding the condition of the Property. Assignee accepts the assignment with full knowledge that the Property is being purchased "AS-IS".

9. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Assignment, together with the Original Agreement, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations or agreements.

10. GOVERNING LAW. This Assignment shall be governed by the laws of the State of {{property_state}}.
